Privacy Policy for Respirare

Last Updated: February 2026

Data Collection

Respirare does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your breathing patterns and sessions are stored in your private iCloud account. This data is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Purchase information is handled securely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any information, there are no special considerations for children's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
